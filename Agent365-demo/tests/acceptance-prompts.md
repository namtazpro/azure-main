## How to use this pack

Run each prompt against the v1 agent and capture:

* Selected intent.
* Invoked skill sequence.
* Coverage status.
* Citation completeness.
* Any clarification requests.

## Jurisdiction identification and constraints

1. We are designing a 9-story residential building in Toronto. What fire safety and egress constraints apply at schematic stage?
2. For a primary school in Melbourne, list accessibility constraints we must include before detailed design.
3. For a mixed-use tower in Dubai, what height and setback constraints should we check first?
4. We are planning an outpatient clinic in Singapore. Which life-safety constraints are typically permit-critical?
5. For a warehouse in Rotterdam, identify structural and occupancy constraints for concept design.

## Cross-country comparison

6. Compare office building energy compliance constraints in Germany versus Spain for schematic design.
7. Compare hospital fire compartmentation expectations in Ontario versus California at concept stage.
8. Compare accessibility constraints for public libraries in the United Kingdom and France.
9. Compare high-rise residential egress constraints in Japan and South Korea.
10. Compare seismic design considerations for schools in Chile and New Zealand.

## Pre-permit risk screening

11. Check this summary for red flags in Madrid: 14 floors, mixed-use, 56 m height, two stair cores, underground parking.
12. Pre-permit review for Abu Dhabi office block: 11 floors, curtain wall facade, 2-hour fire separation proposed.
13. Screen this design in Ontario: 8-story elder-care facility, one evacuation elevator, rooftop mechanical penthouse.
14. Flag likely compliance blockers in Singapore for a laboratory building with hazardous storage on level 2.
15. Assess concept risk in Germany for a timber mid-rise residential building with shared corridors.

## Clause explanation and traceability

16. Explain the practical meaning of this egress clause for a hospital project in Texas and how it affects corridor sizing.
17. Explain this accessibility requirement for a public building in Paris and the design implications.
18. Interpret this energy code clause for a Dubai office facade strategy and state any assumptions.
19. Explain this structural load clause for a school project in Tokyo and what must be verified by engineers.
20. Interpret this fire separation clause for mixed-use development in London and list required follow-up checks.

## Pass criteria

* Agent selects the expected intent for each prompt.
* Response includes regulation edition and amendment date where applicable.
* Response avoids uncited legal assertions.
* Unsupported jurisdictions are clearly marked with next-step guidance.
* High-severity risks are explicit and actionable.
